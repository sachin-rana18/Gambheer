What is pahadi food

The himachali dham dishes is a popular feast served at local markets, weddings, and other special events. This traditional food of himachal pradesh is a fusion of several pahadi dishes such as- Dal, Rice, Madra, Kadi, Rajmah, Khatta, Badi, and Meetha, all served in one single platter.
